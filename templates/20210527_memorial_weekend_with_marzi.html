Summitted Hood on Sunday

The weather was perfect (58-32). Slept for two hours in the parking lot (timberlodge), started hiking up 1:20 am, wore crampons pretty much from the beginning, it gets steeper and steeper and by the time we reached the sulphur part it was the steepest. Ice fall is real and helmet is a must. Someone dies near the summit in the same day we were there. We got to the summit by 6:20, started hiking down by 6:50, reached the car by 9:30.

It got real crowded when we were close to the summit. There were lines and lines of people. We were lucky to be among the first ones to summit!

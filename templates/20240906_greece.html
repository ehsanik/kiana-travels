Went to ferry terminal. So here is the deal:

Seajet: expensive, small, fast, choppy - 3.5 hours to Naxos

Blue Star: cheaper but not so much, really big, takes forever, less choppy - 5.5 hours

Plane: 45 min, costs a little more. If it's windy might be your best bet
